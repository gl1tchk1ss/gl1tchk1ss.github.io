GhostPost is an independent, unofficial catalog of correspondence sent to Luigi Mangione that he has received and painstakingly transcribed into a handwritten catalog. The goal of this projectis to preserve transparency, make the data more accessible, and document mail interactions. As in the original catalog, each entry is documented using initials, ZIP suffixes, postmark dates, and basic metadata‚Äînever full names or letter contents.


Luigi Mangione is an inmate at MDC Brooklyn Prison as well as a public figure. This project was developed by ph1shcr0w.

GhostPost is not affiliated with Luigi Mangione, his legal team, or any campaign. It is an archival community effort to document postal transparency and support free expression.

All data is sourced from the FAQ section of Luigi Mangione's Legal Defense Information website, which is maintained by his legal team.
